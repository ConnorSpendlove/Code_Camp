About MochiFox

A gentle companion for your mental wellness journey

Our Mission

MochiFox was created to help you prioritize your mental health through small, manageable daily tasks. We believe that self-care doesn't have to be overwhelmingâ€”sometimes the smallest actions can make the biggest difference. By tracking your mood and completing simple tasks, you're not just caring for a virtual pet; you're caring for yourself. A fun fact is that this project was created in just 24 hours for a CodeCamp Competition!

How It Works

Track Your Mood

Select how you're feeling each day. Your mood helps us suggest the most helpful tasks for you.

Complete Tasks

Engage with small, achievable self-care activities tailored to your current emotional state.

Care for Your Pet

Watch your virtual pet thrive as you complete tasks. Their happiness reflects your self-care progress.

Our Values

  • Gentleness: We believe in being kind to yourself, especially on difficult days.
  • Accessibility: Self-care should be simple and available to everyone, regardless of their circumstances.
  • Progress, Not Perfection: Every small step counts, and there's no pressure to be perfect.
  • Privacy: Your data stays on your device. Your journey is yours alone.

Get in Touch

Have questions, suggestions, or feedback? We'd love to hear from you! Your input helps us make MochiFox better for everyone.

Remember: This tool is designed to support your wellness journey, but it's not a replacement for professional mental health care. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please reach out to a mental health professional or crisis hotline.
